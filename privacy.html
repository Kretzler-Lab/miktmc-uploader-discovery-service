MiKTMC Data Lake Uploader Privacy Notice

[Updated: April 16, 2024]

Overview

The MiKTMC Data Lake Uploader is administered by the Michigan Kidney Translational Medicine Core (miktmc.org) and maintains the content under the upload.miktmc.org domain. This policy applies to site content at upload.miktmc.org, but not necessarily to additional sites in the miktmc.org domain.

For questions or concerns about this policy, please email our website administrator:curegn-redcap@med.umich.edu

MiKTMC collects or has access to the following information from you with your use of the Uploader:

Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third parties. Individually identifiable information may be shared as required by MiKTMC, The CureGN Consortium, The Neptune Consortium, or by law, including sharing with government agencies to aid in an investigation.
